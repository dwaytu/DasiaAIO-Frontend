# SENTINEL Feature Catalog

## Identity and Access

- Role model: `superadmin`, `admin`, `supervisor`, `guard`
- Guard self-registration with approval gating
- Email verification and password reset lifecycle
- Refresh-token rotation and revocation-aware sessions
- Backend-enforced legal consent state on protected routes

## Personnel and Scheduling

- Shift creation and assignment workflows
- Guard check-in/check-out attendance tracking
- No-show detection and replacement coordination
- Availability management and operational oversight

## Firearms, Permits, and Maintenance

- Firearm inventory and status management
- Issuance and return tracking
- Active and overdue allocation visibility
- Permit lifecycle and training records
- Preventive and corrective maintenance workflows

## Vehicle and Trip Operations

- Armored car inventory and allocation
- Driver assignment and trip lifecycle management
- Vehicle maintenance scheduling and completion records

## Incident, Support, and Notifications

- Incident creation and active incident monitoring
- Support ticket submission and response workflows
- Notification feed with unread counts and mark-read actions

## Command Center and Tracking

- Live map and telemetry ingestion
- WebSocket snapshot stream with polling fallback continuity
- Client-site and geofence-aware monitoring
- Movement reconstruction and patrol visibility workflows

## Analytics and AI-Assisted Operations

- Trend and reliability dashboards
- Predictive risk indicators (permit, maintenance, no-show)
- Deterministic AI endpoints for classification and summaries

## Platform and Runtime Features

- Shared frontend codebase for web, desktop, and Android
- Runtime-aware update prompts and download routing
- Unified backend API contracts across all runtimes
